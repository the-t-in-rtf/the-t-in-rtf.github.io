Product Type:
eye control

Areas of Application:
people after a stroke or who have suffered from a different cause a TBI and / or people with speech disorder in a vegetative state, amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) or Locked-in syndrome (LIS)

Description:
The Eyeblinker 2 is a system by which by means of a sensor glasses on the blink of an eye spoken sentences can be reproduced. The sensor glasses is connected to an electronics box that is connected to a notebook computer. On the computer a special software for the selection of different sentences spoken and the speech itself runs. Alternatively the sensor glasses can also use a proximity sensor.
In addition to the communication aid is the Eyeblinker 2, an emergency call to the maintainer possible.

Features / Components:
- detection of blink via sensor goggles with infrared sensor
- Use of own glasses by attaching the infrared sensor possible
- for individuals who are able to move a hand, instead of the special glasses a hand sensor can be used
- individually by the manufacturer in accordance with the wishes and choices of disabled angepassbare software
- emergency call function is also without switched on notebook possible
