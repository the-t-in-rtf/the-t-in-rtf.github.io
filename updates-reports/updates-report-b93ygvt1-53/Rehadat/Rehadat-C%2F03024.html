Product Type:
Input modification for mouse control

Areas of Application:
- allows control of a computer mouse with head movements and facial gestures

Description:
With the DIPAX HeadMouse3D you can control a PC or laptop with head movements and facial gestures (Kopfmaus, HeadMouse). A gesture signals the computer whether a mouse click is triggered or a program is to be opened.
No additional software is required to perform certain mouse clicks, For example, a double-click or key combinations. In addition, the additionally available depth information of the 3D camera provides a much higher degree of accuracy and a much greater freedom of movement for the user than with conventional systems with a reflector point or webcam.
The mouse pointer is moved over the screen by means of head movements; If head movements are not possible to the user, it is also sufficient to move the mouse pointer to the desired position with alternative face gestures (for example, chin movement).

In addition, this head control can also be used as a joystick or game controller.
