Product Type:
keyboard

Areas of Application:
This keyboard series can be used in head and mouth writers, people with dysmelias, pronounced cerebral or central movement disorders or spastisichen paralysis. The keyboard is available with or without numeric keypad and has latched switching functions

Features / Components:
- Key spacing compared to the standard keyboard enlarged
- directly without any additional software on the computer connected
- different time parameters (delay time, keystroke repetition) can be adjusted individually
- adjustable Einfinger function
- latching for Alt, AltGr, Shift, Ctrl latching adjustable as unique, permanently or not
- status display for latching function LED
- switchable key quit sound
- macro function for storing up to 55 functions
- connection option for second keyboard
- user-specific parameters remain in the keyboard stored

Versions:
keyboard Mercurius without numeric keyboard
- case size: 410 x 175 mm
- front height: 18 mm
- rear height: 35 mm
- weight: 3.4 kg
- enlarged keypad: 400 x 140 mm

keyboard Mercurius with integrated numeric keypad
- case size: 510 x 175 mm
- front height: 18 mm
- rear height: 35 mm
- weight: 4.2 kg
- magnified keypad: 500 x 140 mm

External numeric keypad Mercurius:
- case size: 130 x 155 mm
- front height: 18 mm
- rear height: 33 mm
- weight: 0.8 kg
- enlarged keypad: 85 x 105 mm

Technical Specifications:
Case size and weight: depending on the design
keys Diameter: 19 mm
key travel: 1,6 mm short-stroke keys with defined pressure point
key spacing: 23 mm (from the center of the keys)
actuation force: approx 0.7 Newton
key layout: corresponds to standard MF2 keyboard
keys life: more than 50 million operations
current consumption: 8 - 25 mA, type: 12 mA at 5 V +/- 5 percent
positioning: -20 degrees Celsius to 60 degrees Celsius, operating: 0 degrees Celsius to 50 degrees Celsius
humidity: maximum 80 percent
connection: 6-pin mini-DIN connector (PS / 2)
cable length: 2.5 m
Manufacturer warranty: 3 years

Scope of Delivery:
1 Foot writing keyboard Mercurius
1 Adapter (DIN keyboard connector to mini DIN keyboard jack)
1 Adapter (PS / 2 to USB)
1 User Manual

Options / Accessories:
- external numeric keypad
- Tripod telescopic sensor, movable in all directions by 2 ball heads, attachment to table edge
- hinged feet
- All types of keyboards are also available with extra large lettering available, optionally with black engraving on bright button cap or with white lettering on black button cap

The keyboard described above corresponds to the standard product range of the company Gorlo and Todt. On request adjustments of the keyboard to the disability or special designs for non PC-compatible equipment are possible
