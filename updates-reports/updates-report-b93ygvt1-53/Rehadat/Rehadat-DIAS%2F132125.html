Areas o Application:

Description:
The software has a screenreader as well as support for braille lines. Dolphin ScreenReader helps people with blindness to independently access their computers for work and education.
In addition, there are all variants on mobile USB sticks.

- extensive screenreader with a wide range of settings
- incl. German and British-English voice
- Product variants: Version as CD or USB version (for Windows 7, 8.1 and 10)

- Text Style Announcement
- Read from here - tool
- Language when completing forms on web pages
- Language during the login process
- Fast and accurate Braille output.
- Selection of computer or shorthand braille (full or shorthand).
