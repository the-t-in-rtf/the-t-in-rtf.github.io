Name ZoomText ImageReader
Description ZoomText Image Reader is a text recognition software that makes printed text into text accessible for people with visual impairments. Books, magazines, business documents and more are simple read aloud. Put the text in the document scanner, press on 'read' and few seconds later, the text is displayed in high contrast and read aloud through a natural reading voice. In addition to printed text on paper Image Reader can record text and read images, clipboard directly from the computer screen. Ideal for graphic text documents and web pages. ZoomText Image Reader is ideal for use with ZoomText Magnifier or Magnifier / Reader. Image Reader also installs itself as a standalone product or in combination with other magnification programs and screenreader Features Image Reader uses a high-Machineresolution translation-document camera and scanner ABBYY OCR software to quickly and accurately taking all types of printed documents. Take up and read in two steps: Place the document under the document camera scanner and press the scan. Automatic reading of scanned documents Enter the text or increases in high contrast Reads aloud in natural sounding voices. Start and stop text. Navigate in the text.
