Product Type:
head mouse

Areas of Application:
- Mouse control by means of head movements
- suitable for tetraplegics, ALS patients and other disabilities, where a limited or missing manual motor control prevents the control of conventional standard equipment.

Description:
The emitted infrared rays are emitted by the reflector and received by the camera eye of the device. The electronics of the device evaluates these signals and calculates the coordinates of the mouse pointer position on the screen. The mouse pointer is affected by every small change in the reflector.

Features / Components:
- enables wireless control of the mouse arrow with low head movements
- allows the computer to be operated without a hand movement
- Device components: device with infrared transmitter and receiver, attachable reflector point
- Reflector point is attached to the head with plastic film:
- Plastic film foil can be worn, for example, on the eyeglass bridge or on the forehead
- no further equipment at the head necessary
- The unit with the transmitter and receiver is placed on or under the screen
- Mouse click can be generated by a connected simple sensor
- the standard keyboard can be completely replaced in conjunction with a screen keyboard

Technical Specifications:
Dimensions: 53 mm x 38 mm x 9.5 mm
Connections:
USB for connection to PC or communication aid with dynamic display
Mono mini jack for connecting simple sensors for mouse clicks
Integrated infrared receiver for wireless connection of keys

Scope of Delivery:
USB cable, reflector points, holder for notebooks

Options / Accessories:
- Headmouse Bundle Clickmaster: Software for triggering the mouse click after an adjustable period of time
- Headmouse Dragger: Software for triggering the mouse click after an adjustable period of time
- Mounting bracket
- Reflection points
