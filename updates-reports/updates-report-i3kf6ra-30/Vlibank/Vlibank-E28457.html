Additional Information
- Magic Pro is magnification software for Windows. There is a version with and without speech output.
- Both 32 bit and 64 bit versions of Windows are supported.
- There is also a special 'Scripting Edition' available to make non-current application programs more accessible and to optimise the performance performance in networks.
- Between 1 and 2 are 9 selectable levels of magnification.
- Connection of a second monitor is supported.
- To enhance contrast, there are several options, choose front and background color and set a monochrome image.
- Large text can be read.
- The scripts for the screen reader Jaws and Magic are compatible with each other since Magic and Jaws are from the same manufacturer.
- Magic Pro has different magnification modes meant for maintaining an overview and also to achieve a workable magnification.
- The mouse pointer can be adjusted in size and color.
