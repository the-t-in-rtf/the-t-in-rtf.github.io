Name The Everyday-Life Reading & Writing Binder 1
Description The Everyday-Life Reading &amp; Writing (Binder 1) is a 3-ring open and close binder that is an educational tool or resource designed to help children with Autism develop reading skills along with everyday life skills, which special learners need to practice in order to master. Made for easy for copying. Each section is tabbed with a label. There is a CD that has printable pages. Some of the tasks covered in this binder include: reading the newspaper, instructions, schedules, websites, and emails. Many of the pages are also filled with fun activities for learning. There is also an answer key in the back of the book. 160 pages.Literacy level: Grade 3 through 5.
