Name Ram Leg Mount For The Apple Ipad & Ipad 2 (Model Ram-Bm-L1-Ap8U)
Description The RAM Leg Mount for the Apple iPad &amp; iPad 2, model RAM-BM-L1-AP8U, is a stand for a tablet computer designed for use by individuals with use of only one hand or upper extremity disabilities. The system comes with two straps for tight support and a cradle for the iPad made of high strength composite materials. The user can rotate the cradle to view the iPad in either portrait or landscape mode. The cradle is designed to hold an iPad without a case, sleeve or skin.
