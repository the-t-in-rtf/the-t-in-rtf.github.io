Name Tab Grabber Tablet Computer Holder
Description The Tab Grabber Tablet Computer Wheelchair Holder allows wheelchair users the interactive experience of a tablet computer without having to hold the unit. The swivel head of this computer tablet holder adjusts to any orientation for optimal viewing and interactive positioning. The retractable clips of the Tab Grabber will mount anywhere along the edges of the wheelchair, assuring easy access to all the controls. The base clamp of the Tab Grabber is designed to mount to round tubing up to 1 1/8 inch diameter, which will accommodate most any wheelchair frame size. The flexible yet sturdy gooseneck arm will hold the unit in place for touch screen use but can swing out of the way when entering or exiting the chair. DIMENSIONS: 26 inches length x 5 inches width x 5 inches depth. COMPATIBILITY: Apple iPad, Kindle (Amazon), Nook (Barnes &amp; Noble), Samsung Galaxy, Acer Archos, Asus, Coby, Dell, HP Lenovo, Motorola, Toshiba, Velocity, and the Viewsonic. SHIPPING WEIGHT: 2 pounds. COLOR: Black. WARRANTY/GUARANTEE: As stated by the manufacturer
