Name Humpty Dumpty & Friends
Description Humpty Dumpty &amp; Friends is a cause and effect training program designed to provide switch training for children with cognitive or physical disabilities. The program is both visually and orally stimulating with colorful graphics and animation and lively sounds and auditory cues. Hickory Dickory Dock introduces the concepts of in/out and up/down, while Humpty Dumpty introduces on/off and asleep/awake. Children also play along with Hey Diddle Diddle while the cat fiddles and the cow jumps over the moon. COMPATIBILITY: Versions are available for use on Macintosh and IBM and compatible computers. SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS: Macintosh: Macintosh LC or better, System 7 or higher, 256-color monitor, and 8 megabytes (MB) RAM. For IBM and compaitble computers: 386 DX 33 mHz PC or better, Microsoft Windows 3.1 or Windows 95, 256-color monitor, 8 MB RAM, Sound Blaster (see separate entry) or compatible sound card.
