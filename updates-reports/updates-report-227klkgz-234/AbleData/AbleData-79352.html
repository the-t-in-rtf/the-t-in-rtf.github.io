Name Five Green & Speckled Frogs
Description Five Green &amp; Speckled Frogs is a cause and effect training program designed for use with children with cognitive or multiple disabilities. Activities begin with cause and effect and progress to directionality and simple substraction. One of the included games teaches directionality on a number line; as the learner moves the frog in the proper direction on the line, the frog earns the point, but if the frog moves in the wrong direction, a fly earns the point. The other game teaches simple subtraction by having the child move the frog on the number line to answer a simple problem. When the answer is incorrect, the frog jumps down the line to cue the student. This program may be accessed with a mouse, a single switch, a touch screen, or Intellikeys (see separate entry). COMPATIBILITY: For use on Macintosh computers. SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS: Requires an LC III or faster processor, 8 MB RAM, a 256-color monitor, and System 7. OPTIONS: A set of seven Intellikeys overlays is available.
