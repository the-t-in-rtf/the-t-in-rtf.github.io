Description

Reduced keyboards with built-mouse replacement.
The mini keyboards Dr. Seveke are suitable for people with a small range and low power. The keyboards are small and placed close together buttons. The size of the buttons, the middle between that of a reduced standard keyboard, such as the Cherry Mini, and that of a micro keyboard with magnetic stud. The keys are very light and can be excited operable with a finger or a stick in hand, finger, foot or head.
In addition, each of the keyboards built a mouse replacement. There are versions with a small trackball, versions with a touchpad (touch-surface) and versions with keys to the mouse arrow to drive.
The keyboards are connected to the USB port on your PC and works as a standard keyboard and mouse. In addition, the simulator extra mouse buttons, double clicking and dragging. Combination as Alt and Ctrl keys are automatically put down until the next keystroke.
