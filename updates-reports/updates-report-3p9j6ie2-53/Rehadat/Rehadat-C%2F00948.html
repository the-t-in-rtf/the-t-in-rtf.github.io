Features / Components:
- On a screen illustrated buttons can
be selected by sighting - several user interfaces available
- integrated screen keyboard
- integrated communication interface (with pre-sets)
- integrated environment control
- control of an additional Windows PC possible (key and pointer operation)
- system is fully controllable via eye including switching on and off

a special fixed under a screen (infrared) camera reads the users movements from and observed the fixation point of the pupil on the monitor.
Here, the eye is illuminated by a small infrared light-emitting diode, whereby the image forming multiple reflections: on the retina to the cornea, as well as further reflections on the edges of the eyelids

A specially developed software calculates from the reflections on the retina and the cornea continuously both the coordinates of the fixed point. as well as their duration. Longer fixing a button displayed on the screen causes a function associated with the button. The dwell time is adjustable
By tracking the eye movements by means of a camera system, no further means are necessary to the head or body of the user are attached

The user must be able to meet the following requirements:
- The ability to control at least 1 eye
- the ability to keep the head in front of the Eyegaze camera resting
- something literacy understanding

the room should preferably not be shining brightly illuminated to allow a better evaluation of the infrared light and the system should be installed outside light sources
