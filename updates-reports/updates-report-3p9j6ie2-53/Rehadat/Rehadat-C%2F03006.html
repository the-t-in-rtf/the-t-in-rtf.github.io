Product Type:
extensive keyboard

Areas of Application:
This keyboard series can be used in head and mouth writers, people with dysmelias, pronounced cerebral or central movement disorders or spasticity. Also suitable for foot writer and head bar users. The keyboard is supplied with a cover plate for the finger guide, depending on the request, with or without numeric keypad available and has latched switching functions

Features / Components:
- Enlarged keys deepened lying are colored differently and arranged arcuate
- directly without further software to the computer connected
- integrated connector for second keyboard
- adjustable Einfinger function
- latching for Alt, AltGr, Shift, Ctrl as unique, permanently or non-latching adjustable
- status display for latching function LED
- 4 individually adjustable time delay
- user-specific parameters remain in the keyboard stored
- switchable key quit sound
- macro function for storing up to 55 functions
- housing construction: hard aluminum plate with real wood frame

Versions:
extensive keyboard with integrated numeric keypad
- case size: 630 x 325 mm
- height front: 28 mm
- rear height: 60 mm price
- weight 4.9 kg
- as right-handed keyboard or left-handed keyboard available

External numeric keypad Jumbo Maxi:
- case size: 195 x 220 mm
- front height: 28 mm
- rear height: 49 mm
- weight: 1.3 kg

Technical Specifications:
Case size and weight: depending on the design
keys diameter: 25 mm
key spacing: 35 mm (from the center of the keys)
key hub: 1.6mm short travel deepened with tactile feedback
key depression: 3 mm (also available with 6 mm groove available)
actuation force: approx 0.7 Newton
keys life: more than 10 million operations
current consumption: 8 - 25 mA, type: 12 mA at 5 V +/- 5 percent
positioning: -20 degrees Celsius to 60 degrees Celsius, operating: 0 degrees Celsius to 50 degrees Celsius
humidity: maximum 80 percent
connection: 6-pin mini-DIN connector (PS / 2)
cable length: 2.5 m
Manufacturer warranty: 3 years

Scope of Delivery:
1 extensive keyboard Jumbo Maxi
1 Adapter (DIN keyboard connector to mini DIN keyboard jack)
1 Adapter PS / 2 to USB
1 User Manual

Options / Accessories:
- integrated button mouse function can be activated using the cursor keys with dynamic pointer speed and Sticky by left-hold key and double-click button
- external numeric keypad
- All types of keyboards are also available with extra large lettering available
- cover made of Plexiglas or black aluminum

the keyboard described above corresponds to the standard product range of the company Gorlo and Todt. On request adjustments of the keyboard to the disability or special designs for non PC-compatible equipment are possible
